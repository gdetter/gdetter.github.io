Junior Year

RedZone Robotics Co-op

I completed three co-op rotations at RedZone Robotics. While at RedZone, I had the opportunity to work on many projects including autonomous manhole inspection systems, mineshaft inspection robots, and various sewer inspection robots. During my time at RedZone, I got to brainstorm, design, prototype, manufacture, refine, maintain, and trouble shoot all kinds of electromechanical systems.
